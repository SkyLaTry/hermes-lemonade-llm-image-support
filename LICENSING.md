# Licensing (SkyLaTry Hermes plugins)

Plain-language summary of the [SkyLaTry Shared Source License](LICENSE). The LICENSE file is the legal text; this page is for humans.

## What you can do for free

- **Personal and non-commercial use** — use, modify, and share the software with attribution.
- **Small commercial use** — commercial use is allowed if your **Attributable Revenue** from products or services that materially depend on the software stays **below USD 25,000 per calendar year**, with attribution.

## Attribution (always required)

Keep copyright and license notices in the code. In public distributions, docs, UI, or marketing that feature the software or a derivative, include a notice like:

```

https://github.com/SkyLaTry/<repository-name>
Copyright (c) SkyLaTry — licensed under the SkyLaTry Shared Source License
```

## Commercial use above the revenue threshold

If Attributable Revenue in a calendar year reaches or exceeds **USD 25,000**:

1. **Notify SkyLaTry** within 30 days of crossing the threshold.
2. Pay **10%** of Attributable Revenue **above** the threshold for that calendar year.
3. Provide reasonable written revenue reporting when requested (at most once per quarter).

Until those obligations are met for the applicable period, commercial rights under the license are suspended for that period.
